your squat is unique to your body

your femur length, torso length, and ankle mobility determine exactly how much forward lean you need to stay balanced. fulcrum measures your proportions with your camera, then computes your personalized squat form using physics.

you'll stand in a t-pose for calibration, enter your height and weight, and see an animated model of your ideal squat mechanics. nothing leaves your device — all processing runs locally in your browser.
